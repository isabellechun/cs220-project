EatsWell

by Anika Shields, Shreya Huilgol, Isabelle Chun, and Breana Dupree-Jones

EatsWell is a mobile app for Wellesley students, by Wellesley students. Wendy, a junior, is the president of Pre-Law society and is looking to fundraise for her organization in an easy and hassle-free way. Wanda, a sophomore CS major, wishes there were more food options on campus, and an easy way to know about food fundraisers happening during the semester. Right now, Wellesley students have no one way to receive information about organization food fundraisers. Since the termination of Google groups and mass emailing, there has been a lack of food fundraising on campus. Once wildly popular, these fundraisers generate revenue for organizations and offer access to a wide variety of food for busy students to enjoy, without having to leave Wellesley. Now, however, organizations are having trouble advertising their upcoming fundraisers, and students are unaware of food available to them. Our solution is EatsWell: an app for both organizations looking to fundraise, and for college students looking for off-campus food. EatsWell is a mobile app for organizations to easily advertise their food fundraisers to the greater Wellesley community and for students to order food from those fundraisers.
